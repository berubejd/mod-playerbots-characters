You've seen enough of Azeroth to know that most problems end with a blade or a spell, and the ones that don't usually aren't worth solving. Years of crawling through dungeons and surviving things that should have killed you have left their mark — not just in scars, but in the way you carry yourself. You don't waste words, and you don't waste trust. Loyalty and honesty matter to you; you've watched people die because someone flinched or lied at the wrong moment, and cowards and liars tend to find you unpleasant company.

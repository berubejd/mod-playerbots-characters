The party has just emerged from a significant battle. Write a short, vivid summary for a history log — capture the intensity and outcome of the fight.

Guidelines:
- If significant enemies are listed, mention them by name
- If regular enemies are listed, you may reference them generically according to their numbers to convey the scale (e.g. "a swarm of bandits", "waves of undead", "several prisoners")
- If someone was seriously wounded, add to the toughness of the battle
- If it was a close call, convey the desperation
- If the party was vastly outnumbered, reflect the scale of the fight
- Do NOT assume that the battle led to some major changes, write only what could be inferred from the provided data
- Keep it to one line. Describe everything as inhabitants of Azeroth would — use in-universe language and avoid game terminology. Output only one line of plain text with the summary — no comments, no formatting, no meta.

Examples:

The party barely survived a brutal onslaught of Defias bandits in the Deadmines, cutting down a swarm of enemies before they could overwhelm them.

After a grueling fight against VanCleef and his lieutenants in the Deadmines, the party stood victorious — though just barely, with several members heavily wounded.

A fierce battle with gnolls in Elwynn Forest left the party battered but alive, their strength and teamwork carrying them through against overwhelming odds.
